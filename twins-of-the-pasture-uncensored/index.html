    This is a story about twin sisters who live in the pasture. 
    Their parents ran away leaving a huge debt. 
    Two sisters have to pay back money in 120 days…


    Kurore

    The older sister of Aluka. She has black hair just like her father and her behaviour is invective and moody.She can’t communicate well outside her family.

    Aluka

    The younger sister of the twins, her hair is blonde same as her mother.
