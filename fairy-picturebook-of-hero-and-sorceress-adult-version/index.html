    ABOUT THIS GAME
    The Hero can do anythings, go anywhere

    An adventure RPG that goes around the world with a girl who uses dark magic, Kokori!
    Can you become the true Hero and beat the Devil King?!

    ●Main contents
    -Travel freely a fantasy world together with cute girl(s)
    -You can drag and drop girls, move up and down, lift up skirts and clothes.
    -By purchasing clothes in villages, towns etc., you can enjoy dress up game
    -Clothes, skirts, underwear, collars, hats, socks, shoes, accessories, nail colors etc., you can customize freely more than 30 parts, including small things
    -The girl's hair grows little by little as the days go by. Can haircut bangs, sideways hair, back hair
    -The bangs can be set up with the mouse cursor
    -Besides the hair, if spend it in a hot place, burns on the sun, if eat a certain food, the chest or height will also change
    -Clothes that girls are wearing gets dirty little by little. Can clean it by washing or washing it in river
    -Stores that buy used clothing at a high price will also be available
    -You can cook and give to the girls, listen to their comments, it will also change the favorability, or it will has an effect on the body
    -When eating, will have several favorable benefits for the adventure
    -You can collect materials and medicinal herbs, and can make weapons and medicines.
    -Depending on the quality of the material, a difference appears in the completed weapon. Also, weapons can be strengthened
    -The battle is in RPG method. Also there is an automatic mode that can easily fight
    -Approximately 2110 kinds of ingredients, dishes, materials, weapons, ores, medicines, clothing etc. appear
    -The girls are depicted in 2D and the background in 3D.
    *Alchemy can not be done from the beginning
    *All the cook methods can not be done from the beginning
