    ABOUT THIS GAME
    This is a very strange story about a little robot that wakes in a very strange place with a very strange purpose. Everything that surrounds him is really dangerous. He doesn’t know why, but something inside is telling him that he should overcome all these traps…
    But why is he doing this? He doesn’t understand what is going on until he gets in a bar.
    Now he is not a robot anymore…
    So the basic concept is:
    The First type of gameplay (platformer) – our hero is locked in the body of a robot and he has to go through all puzzle chambers.
    Occasionally, he will awaken in his real body. And this is…
    The second type of gameplay (adventure/visual novel) – here our hero will interact with other characters. Let’s call it a storytelling part of the game.
